Initiatives.

our model.

Each year, we focus on one aspect of racial issues at New Trier we would like to work on and formulate a detailed, robust initiative proposal for improving that aspect. We finalize the proposal by mid-July, try to secure administration cooperation by the end of July, and begin implementation in August.

Our research branch works to track the progress and evaluate the performance of our initiative, conducting two surveys throughout the school year (one in the fall, one in the spring) and publishing an annual report at the end of the school year.
