Initiatives.

our model.

Each year, we focus on one aspect of diversity at New Trier and formulate a detailed, robust proposal. We finalize the proposal by mid-July, try to secure administration cooperation by the end of July, and begin implementation in August.

Our research branch works to track the progress and evaluate the performance of our initiatives, conducting two surveys throughout the school year (one in the fall, one in the spring) and publishing an annual report of our findings.
