Helping New Trier prepare its students for a racially complex world.

The New Trier Coalition for Diversity's mission is to provide an effective, unified voice for all races/ethnicities in the New Trier student and alumni community, and to use our unified voice to promote productive discussions with the New Trier High School administration on issues of race and diversity in education.

about us

In the wake of George Floyd’s death, New Trier has once again been starkly reminded of the importance of diversity, inclusion, and education around racial issues. Though we respect New Trier High School’s existing and ongoing efforts, the school has considerable room for progress. We feel that New Trier would benefit from regular discourse with an external organization holding the administration accountable for concrete, effective action.


​

We are that external organization. As a long-term solution to ensure that student and alumni voices reach the administration, we created the New Trier Coalition for Diversity. Our mission is to provide an effective, unified voice for all races/ethnicities in the New Trier student and alumni community. We are composed of an equal number of Asian/Pacific Islander, Black, Hispanic/Latinx, Jewish, Middle Eastern, Multiracial, and White representatives who create proposals and discuss ways to make New Trier a more inclusive environment.
