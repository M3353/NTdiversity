Helping New Trier prepare its students for a racially complex world.

Our mission is to provide an effective, unified voice for all races/ethnicities in the New Trier student and alumni community, and to use our unified voice to promote productive discussions with the New Trier High School administration on issues of race and diversity in education.

about us

In the wake of George Floyd’s death, New Trier has once again been starkly reminded of the importance of diversity, inclusion, and education around racial issues. Though we respect New Trier High School’s existing and ongoing efforts, the school has considerable room for progress. We feel that New Trier would benefit from regular discourse with an external organization holding the administration accountable for concrete, effective action.

Get involved.

We are currently seeking to establish regular meetings with the New Trier administration on issues of race and diversity in education. We need to have the infrastructure in place for sustained and lasting change. Having an organized body meet with the administration is the first step in getting our foot in the door.
